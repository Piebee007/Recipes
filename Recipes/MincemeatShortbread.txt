Mincemeat Shortbread
Category: Dessert
Prep Time: 0:45
Cook Time: 0:35
Serves: 16
Ingredients: 
75g semolina
125g caster sugar
200g plain flour
200g butter
800g Mincemeat
Sablee Pastry:
125g Plain flour
100g butter
100g caster sugar
1 egg yolk
Instructions:
To make the Sablee Pastry:
Chop the butter into small cubes.
In a bowl, rub together the flour, caster sugar, and butter until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
Mix in the egg yolk until the dough comes together.
Place the dough in the freezer for about 30 minutes or in the fridge for at least an hour to chill.
To make the Mincemeat Shortbread:
Preheat the oven to 180Â°C and prepare a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
In a separate bowl, combine the butter, caster sugar, and plain flour for the shortbread mixture.
Rub the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Take the chilled Sablee Pastry out of the freezer/fridge and roll it out to fit the baking tray
Place the rolled-out Sablee Pastry onto the prepared baking tray.
Spread an even layer of mincemeat on top of the pastry.
Crumble the shortbread mixture over the mincemeat layer, covering it evenly.
Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 30-35 minutes or until the shortbread turns golden brown.
Remove from the oven and allow it to cool before slicing into bars or squares.
Serve and enjoy the delicious Mincemeat Shortbread as a delightful dessert.
