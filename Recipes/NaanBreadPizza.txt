Naan Bread pizza
Category: Peperoni
Prep Time: 0:10
Cook Time: 0:05
Serves: 2
Ingredients:
2 Naan Breads
4 tbsp tomato puree
1tsp Mixed herbs
1tsp Garlic Granules
100g Cheese, grated
100g Peperoni
(Optional) Any other toppings
Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 180Â°C
Add the tomatoe puree into a bowl and add a splash of water. Then add a sprinkle of salt, pepper, mixed herbs and garlic granules. Mix all together
Spread the sauce to the naan breads. Add the grated cheese, peperoni (or other toppings)
Put in the oven for 4-5 minutes, until the cheese is all melted
Serve with any additional sides
