Lemon Drizzle Cake
Category: Dessert
Prep Time: 0:10
Cook Time: 0:40
Serves: 8
Ingredients:
6oz Self Raising Flour
5oz Castor Sugar
4oz Butter
2 Eggs
0.5 Cup Milk
 Grated Rind of Lemon
Syrup:0.33 Cup Granulated Sugar
3 Lemons Juiced
Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
Cream the butter and sugar. 
Then add the eggs, plain flour, milk and lemon rind and mix until combined.
Pour into a lined loaf tin.
Bake for 40 minutes in the oven.
Whilst the cake is in the oven, start making the syrup. To do that mix the sugar and lemon juice together.
Once the cake is cooked and still hot in the tin, prick all over with a skewer and pour syrup in the holes.
Don't take out of the tin until it's cold.
