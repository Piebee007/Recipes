Toffee Sauce
Category: Dessert
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Serves: 4
Ingredients:
Sauce:
125g Butter
100g Light muscovado sugar
6tbsp double cream
Fruit:
1 Apple, sliced
1 Banana, sliced
1 kiwi, sliced
Hanfull of strawberries, halved
Handfull of grapes
Instructions:
Place the butter and sugar in a saucepan and heat, stirring gently until the butter has melted and sugar has dessolved.
Add the cream to the saucepan and bring to the boil. Boil for 1-2 minutes then leave to cool slighly.
Serve with an assortment of fruit to dip.
