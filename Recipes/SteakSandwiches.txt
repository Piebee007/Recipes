Steak Sandwiches
Category: Steak
Prep Time: 0:10
Cook Time: 0:20
Servings: 2
Ingredients:
2 Steaks
2 Torpedo Buns
1 Onion, chopped
1 Mozzeralla Ball
2tbsp Butter
1tbsp Parsley, finely chopped
2 Garlic cloves, finely chopped
Instructions:
Pat the steaks dry and season generously.
Combine the garlic and parsely with the butter and spread on the torpedo buns.
Put the onions on low heat with a splace of water. Cover with a lid until caramelised.
Then cook the steaks on high heat according to your desired doneness, turning once. The cooking time will depend on the thickness of the steaks and your preference. 
Once cooked, remove the staks from the pan and wrap them in foil. ALlow to rest for at least 5 minutes.
Add the Mozeralla ball to the caramelised onions and allow to melt.
Thinly slice the rested steaks and place them on the torpedo buns.
Top the steak with the melted mozzarella and onion mixture.
Serve the steak sandwiches warm and enjoy!
