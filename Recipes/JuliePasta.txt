Julie Pasta
Category: Pasta
Prep Time: 0:5
Cook Time: 0:35
Serves: 4
Ingredients:
150g Salami
1 Red Onion, sliced
1 Garlic Clove, diced
4tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
400g Chopped Tomato Tin
20g Fresh Basil, Chopped
300g Rigatoni
1 Mozzarella Ball, Sliced
Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 190°C.
Heat the olive oil in a frying pan, and fry half the salami (cut into quarters) until quite crisp. Add the onion and garlic and cook slowly until the onion is soft.
Add the balsamic vinegar, chopped tomatoes, crème fraiche and basil.
Mix together, season to taste, and simmer for a few minutes.
Cook the rigatoni until 'al dente. Drain, and tip into a baking dish and pour over the sauce.
Lay the mozzarella over the top with the rest of the salami. 
Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes until golden.
