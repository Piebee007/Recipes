Biscoff Cake
Category: Dessert
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Serves: 10
Ingredients:
400g Biscoff Paste
3 Eggs
2tsp baking powder
100g white chocolate
Instructions:
Preheat an oven to 160Â°C. 
In a bowl, mix 350g of the biscoff paste, the eggs and the baking powder until well combined.
Line a baking tin with baking paper and spread the mixture evenly in the tin.
Bake for 18-20 minutes until the cake is cooked through.
Once it is out of the oven, melt the chocolate and any left over biscoff spread and cover over the cake.
Allow the cake to cool, then cut it into squares for serving.
