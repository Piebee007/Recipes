Raspberry Layer Cheesecake
Category: Dessert
Prep Time: 0:15
Cook Time: 0:02
Serves: 8
Ingredients:
100g Digestives, crushed
40g Margarine, melted
24g Sachets Raspberry jelly
200g Light Soft Cheese
200g Raspberry Yogurt
Instructions:
Mix the crushed digestives with the melted margarine and press into a 20cm (8in) springform tin. 
Chill in the freezer for 15 mins.
Meanwhile, dissolve half of the jelly in 150ml (50z) boiling water, then leave to cool.
Beat the soft cheese and yogurt together, then whisk in the cooled jelly.
Pour onto the biscuit base and chill in the fridge for 30 mins, or until set.
Meanwhile, make up the rest of the raspberry jelly with 450ml boiling water and leave to cool. Pour over the set cheesecake and chill for 1 hr, or until firm
Carefully unmould the cheesecake, cut into 8 slices and serve topped with fresh raspberries.
