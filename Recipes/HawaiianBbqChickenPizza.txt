Hawaiian BBQ Chicken Pizza
Category: Chicken
Prep Time: 0:30
Cook Time: 0:15
Serves: 2
Ingredients:
2 Plain Flatbread
3tbsp BBQ Sauce
1 Chicken Breast
1 Red Onion, thinly sliced
2 Pineapple slices, grilled and cut thinly
125g Grated Mozzarella
Instructions:
Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 180Â°C
Cook the chicken in the oven for 20-25 minutes (optionally brush some BBQ sauce on the chicken for added flavour).
Once cooked, allow the chicken to rest for a few minutes before cutting into small, bite-sized chunks.
Place the flatbreads on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
Spread the BBQ sauce evenly on the flatbread.
Then put half of the cheese on top of the BBQ cause.
Then add all of the toppings.
Finally add the rest of the cheese.
Bake the pizzas in the oven for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
